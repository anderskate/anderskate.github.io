Instructions
You MUST bring:
  • A printed copy of your appointment letter
  • Your DS-160 confirmation page
  • Your current passport and all old passports
  • Additional documents may apply on the medical examination (Immigrant visa)
  • One photograph (if you did not upload your photo to DS-160, see the webpage: http://www.ustraveldocs.com/pl/pl-niv-photoinfo.asp
We look forward to seeing you at the Embassy / Consulate. 

For your safety and convenience, we ask you at the time of your appointment, and not earlier. Please note, however, that some have the same time as you. All visa applicants must go through security screening before entering the Consular Section. 
    You MAY bring:
    • A folder with application related documents and a small bag.
    • Cellphone * (it must be stored at security area)
    * The embassy accepts no liability for cellphones left with the security staff. 

    What NOT to bring:
    • Large shoulder bags / purses (larger than the size of A-4 format page) and zip folders.
    • Luggage, such as suitcases, backpacks, briefcases, and other travel bags.
    • Battery-operated or electronic devices such as cameras, laptops, tablets, recording devices or portable music players.
    • Food and drink
    • Sealed envelopes or packages, except for sealed envelopes.
    • Cigarettes, cigars, match boxes, lighters
    • Sharp objects such as scissors, pen knives or nail files
    • Weapons or explosive materials of any kind
    Other items may be prohibited at the discretion of security staff.
    • There is no facility at the Embassy/ Consulate to store prohibited items (aside from cellphones). You will need to make arrangements for other items before entering.
    • Only customers with a confirmed appointment will be admitted to the Consular Section.
    • Unfortunately, there is no parking in the building.
